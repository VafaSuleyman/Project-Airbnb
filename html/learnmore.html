Kissonerga, Kipr

Villa Vista is an exceptional luxury villa located right on the sea front with a small sandy beach literally a couple of minutes walk away along with bar and restaurant. A few minutes further walk there are more restaurants and a small supermarket for your needs. Very tranquil with the most amazing views of the sunset especially from the luxurious rooftop hot tub.

Under a 10 minute drive is the main Coral Bay strip full of restaurants, bars & shops and 2 of the best beaches on the island, Coral Bay Beach & Corallia Beach, a mini amusement park with mini golf course.

Go-karting, quad biking, horse riding, jeep safaris, diving, boat tours, wine tours and golfing including Paphos Zoo and the truly amazing Akamas National Park.
